# FAST Linux Migration: Actionable Checklist for Banks

Based on the project timeline and industry requirements, here is the checklist of key dates and milestones your team needs to track:

## Phase 1: Planning & Nomination (Q1 2026)

- Attend IDT Workshop Session 1 (In-person): Scheduled for March 2026 (Week 2) to cover project background, timeline, and pre-requisites.
- Attend IDT Workshop Session 2 (In-person/Teams): Scheduled for March 2026 (Week 2).
- Attend IDT Workshop Session 3 (Teams): Scheduled for March 2026 (Week 3).
- Attend IDT Workshop Session 4 (Teams): Scheduled for March 2026 (Week 3).
- Finalize IDT Scope: Confirm the testing approach, scope, and test cases by March 2026 (Week 4).

## Phase 2: Preparation & Connectivity (Q3 2026 - Q1 2027)

- IDT Environment Setup: Monitor the setup of the Application IDT environment from September to October 2026.
- Onboard IDT Command Centre: Finalize onboarding by November 2026.
- Execute Connectivity Testing: Participate in mandatory connectivity testing in January 2027 prior to the start of formal IDT testing.

## Phase 3: Execution & Go-Live (Q1 - Q2 2027)

- Industry Development Testing (IDT): Allocate resources for the full testing window from January to April 2027.
- Dress Rehearsal (DRH): Support and participate in the DRH within the IDT environment in early April 2027.
- Go-Live (Weekend 1): Prepare for the Real-Time Application migration in May 2027, accounting for approximately 100 minutes of downtime.
- Go-Live (Weekend 2): Prepare for the G3 Backoffice migration, accounting for approximately 6 hours of downtime.

## Critical Downtime Summary

| Activity | Estimated Downtime | Impact |
|----------|-------------------|--------|
| Online Transactions | ~100 minutes | Real-time transaction processing |
| Rollback (if needed) | ~35 minutes | Service reversion duration |
| Backoffice/Portal | ~6 hours | Non-availability of portal/functional tools |
